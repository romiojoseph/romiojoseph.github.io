BlueSky Trending Topics

BlueSky is becoming the next big thing. A moderated space where people gather to discuss what's happening, free from hate speech and disruptive behavior.

Published on 05 Feb, 2025 Updated on 13 Apr, 2025 in Bluesky

This is a collection of feeds made by @trending.bsky.app on Bluesky. They were created to show what's trending on the platform.

Sometimes, older feeds get archived as snapshots once trends fade. Right now, feeds don’t include a createdAt field—only indexedAt, which shows when the record was first seen. Use the buttons to view posts on Bluesky or search for them on news sites.

This website highlights trending topics on Bluesky. Right now, the only way to explore trends is by visiting the trending account and browsing its feeds.

I wanted to make a calendar view so people could check trends by date. But since the feeds don’t have proper timestamps, I built it this way instead. Maybe I’ll revisit it later with better ideas.

Bluesky feels like the next big space where people can talk about what’s happening, without hate speech or noisy behavior. It’s more like a moderated public square where conversations can stay meaningful.
