Bluesky User Statistics

Enter a Bluesky username to see stats on posts, likes, reposts, and follows. View hashtag, mention, link, and word usage, along with their counts.

Published on 12 Feb, 2025 Updated on 13 Apr, 2025 in Bluesky

Enter any Bluesky username to see stats on posts, likes, reposts, and follows. You’ll also get counts for hashtags, mentions, links, and words. Click on any item to see where it appears.

No login needed. Bluesky is open. But please respect others' privacy when using this tool.

Disclaimer

This tool is a follow-up to Convert Bluesky CAR to JSON. With this, you can view stats for any Bluesky user—posts, likes, reposts, and follows.

As I kept adding features, I saw it could do more. My favorite part is content analysis. It shows what I talk about most, which hashtags I use, and which links I often share.

It’s helpful for people using Bluesky for marketing. But even regular users can check in now and then to get an idea of how they’re doing on the platform.

This all started because I wanted to track my own activity. I know people will use it in their own way. Adding login support might help, but it would also make things more complicated for casual users.
